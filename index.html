OUR STORY

A Legacy of
Pure Gold

Founded in 1890, AAYI has been crafting jewellery that transcends generations. Every piece is born from the hands of master artisans who pour their soul into every curve and setting.

Our commitment is simple — to create jewellery that tells your story, in the most precious metals on Earth.

134 YEARS OF CRAFTSMANSHIP

GOLD · SILVER · DIAMONDS · GEMSTONES

MILESTONES

Our Journey

1890

Founded in Jaipur

Raja Bir Singh established AAYI as a royal goldsmith to the Jaipur court, crafting for maharajas and nobility.

1950

National Recognition

Awarded the National Artisan Award for excellence in goldsmithing by the Government of India.

1990

International Expansion

Opened showrooms in Dubai, London, and Singapore, bringing Indian jewellery to the world.

2025

Digital Luxury

Launched the AAYI digital platform, making handcrafted luxury accessible to customers worldwide.
